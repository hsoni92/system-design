## Overview

Complete, official syllabus from Coursera. The program includes **7 courses** (~140+ hours total; ~6 months at 10 hours/week). It is **100% hands-on**: every course has videos, readings, quizzes, reflections, graded challenges, practice activities, plugins, and templates (Google Workspace + Asana). You build real artifacts (charters, plans, risk registers, RACI charts, backlogs, reports) for your job portfolio. **Heavy AI integration** throughout (Gemini for charters, risks, status updates, meetings, retrospectives, comms; NotebookLM & Gemini Live in the final course).

## 1. Foundations of Project Management (13 hours – 4 modules)

**Skills:** Project Management; Project Management Life Cycle; Agile & Waterfall; Organizational Structure; Change Management; Strategic Thinking; Generative AI for PM.

**Tools:** Google Sheets/Docs/Slides, Asana, generative AI agents.

### Module 1: Embarking on a career in project management

- **Objectives:** Program structure; what PM is; role of a PM; transferring prior skills; PM role types; job-search strategies.
- **Topics/Methods:** Reflection on your experience; career exploration.
- **Assignments:** Reflection: Your project management experience; Test your knowledge quiz; Module 1 Challenge; Entry survey.

### Module 2: Becoming an effective project manager

- **Objectives:** How PMs add value; full responsibilities; core skills (communication, organization, leadership, influence without authority, flexibility, handling ambiguity).
- **Methods:** Cross-functional team work; influencing without authority; common PM myths.
- **AI:** “Boost your project management skills with AI”; “Use generative AI to work smarter and faster”; video “Ben: Driving impact with AI in the workplace”.
- **Assignments:** 3 reflections; 2 knowledge tests; “Identify: Project opportunities and tasks” plugin; Module 2 Challenge.

### Module 3: The project management life cycle and methodologies

- **Objectives:** All 4 phases + tasks; compare methodologies.
- **Methods & topics:**
  - **Phases:** Initiating, Planning, Executing, Closing (detailed tasks in each).
  - Waterfall vs Agile (full comparison).
  - Intro to Lean & Six Sigma.
  - Hybrid approaches.
- **Assignments:** 2 knowledge tests; “Identify: Phases” & “Identify: Methodologies” plugins; Module 3 Challenge.

### Module 4: Organizational structure and culture

- **Objectives:** Structures & impact on PM; culture; PM’s role in change management.
- **Methods:** Classic/Functional, Matrix, Projectized structures; PMO; change management process.
- **Assignments:** 3 knowledge tests; Module 4 Challenge; “Explore: Organizational culture” plugin.

- **Assignments:** Stakeholder analysis & power grid activity; RACI chart activity + exemplar; knowledge test; “Identify: Project role responsibilities” & “Explore: Stakeholder interviews” plugins; Module 3 Challenge.

### Module 4: Utilizing resources and tools for project success

- **Methods:** Project charter components; project proposals; documentation value; tool evaluation.
- **AI:** “Use AI to create a project charter” (dedicated video + activity).

## 3. Project Planning: Putting It All Together (23 hours – 5 modules)

**Skills:** WBS; Gantt charts; Critical Path; Budgeting; Procurement; Risk Management Plan; Communication Plan; Estimation; Kanban.

**AI:** “Identify potential project risks with gen AI”.

### Module 1: Beginning the planning phase

- **Methods:** Kick-off meeting; tasks vs milestones; **Work Breakdown Structure (WBS)**.
- **Assignments:** WBS activities (Parts 1 & 2); milestone assignment; Module 1 Challenge; “Project Plant Pals: Planning” plugin.

### Module 2: Building a project plan

- **Methods:** Time estimation techniques; capacity planning; **Critical Path Method**; **Gantt charts**; **Kanban boards**; planning fallacy avoidance.
- **Assignments:** Build Gantt chart (180 min); Module 2 Challenge.

### Module 3: Managing budgeting and procurement

- **Methods:** Budget components & templates; **Statement of Work (SoW)**; procurement documentation & ethics.
- **Assignments:** Develop project budget activity + exemplar; create SoW activity + exemplar; Module 3 Challenge.

### Module 4: Managing risks effectively

- **Methods:** Risk identification (brainstorming, **fishbone diagram**); types of risk; probability/impact; mitigation strategies (avoid, mitigate, transfer, accept); risk management plan; **ROAM**; single point of failure.
- **AI:** “Identify potential project risks with gen AI”.
- **Assignments:** Create risk management plan (60 min); Module 4 Challenge.

### Module 5: Organizing communication and documentation

- **Methods:** Communication plan (who, what, when, how); central documentation; data storytelling prep.
- **Assignments:** Draft communication plan; organize documents activity; Module 5 Challenge.

## 6. Capstone: Applying Project Management in the Real World (38 hours – 4 modules)

You manage the full *Project Plant Pals* scenario and build a **complete portfolio**.

### Module 1: Initiating a project

- Draft, revise & finalize full project charter (SMART goals, scope, benefits, costs, stakeholder alignment).
- Complete stakeholder analysis.
- **Assignments:** Multiple charter revisions + final charter (180 min).

### Module 2: Building out a project plan

- Identify tasks (documentation research + conversations).
- WBS, milestones, Gantt schedule.
- **Three-point estimating** + confidence ratings + negotiation.
- **Assignments:** Full project plan build.

### Module 3: Maintaining quality

- Define quality standards & indicators.
- Create evaluation/survey questions.
- Facilitate retrospective (participation, accountability, positivity).
- **Assignments:** Quality standards addition; evaluation presentation (180 min); retrospective document.

### Module 4: Effective stakeholder communication

- Escalate problems & connect to goals.
- Closeout report, executive summary, personal closing report.
- **AI:** “Use AI to improve project communications”.
- **Assignments:** Closeout report; executive summary (180 min); personal closing report.

**Portfolio deliverables:** Charter, full plan (WBS/Gantt/risk/comms), quality eval, reports — ready for job applications.
